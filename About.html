Where did tacos come from?

The invention of tacos cannot be attributed to a single individual or moment in history. Instead, tacos are a culinary creation that evolved over centuries, primarily within the indigenous cultures of Mexico, long before Spanish colonization. heir development is a testament to the blending of local traditions and available ingredients, making them a collective invention of Mexican culture. (Source: History Cooperative)
